Behind the scenes

of the UN Millenium Goal Development Report

How much data is behind the MGD report? How many people does it represent?

Use this visualization to explore how much data has been collected about each of the millenium goals. Learn how much of Earth's population is represented in each of data points collected. See for which goals the UN is making progress in collecting more data in recent years.

This visualization can be a companion when reading the report or looking at other visualizations. Use it to investigate if results presented are grounded on a sound basis of data.
